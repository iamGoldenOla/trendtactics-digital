## Key Recommendations:

### 1. **Add Pricing Ranges**
Instead of just packages, consider:
- "Starting from $X/month" or
- "$X - $Y/month" ranges
- Or "Contact for pricing" with package descriptions

### 2. **Clarify Service Scope**
- Specify exact deliverables (e.g., "8 social media posts with graphics")
- Include revision rounds
- Specify response times for support

### 3. **Add Value Indicators**
- Average ROI/ROAS
- Expected results timeline
- Case study links

### 4. **Consider Add-Ons**
- Additional campaigns: +$X per campaign
- Extra platforms: +$X per platform
- Rush delivery: +20-30%

### 5. **Transparency**
- Separate management fees from ad spend (for Facebook Ads)
- One-time vs recurring costs
- Contract terms (month-to-month vs annual)
